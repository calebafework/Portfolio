Blacks In Technology

I recently helped start the Denver chapter of Blacks In Technology. As the Chapter co-lead I help organize events and establish programs locally. The Blacks In Technology Foundation is the largest community of Black people in the technology industry. Through community-focused activities, events and outreach, The Blacks In Technology (BIT) Foundation is “Stomping the Divide” by establishing a blueprint of world-class technical excellence and innovation by providing resources, guidance, networking, and opportunities for members to share their expertise and advance their careers.
